About Petal Pop Co.

Two friends bringing custom curb appeal to Utah homes.

Petal Pop Co. started with two friends, a love for flowers, and yards that slowly became completely full.

What began as a creative outlet quickly turned into a passion for designing colorful, welcoming outdoor spaces that make homes feel special. After years of planting and styling our own flower beds, porch planters, and seasonal spaces, we realized we wanted to help others create that same feeling without the overwhelm of doing it themselves.

We specialize in seasonal annual flower bed styling that brings instant curb appeal, color, and personality to your home. But we also offer custom perennial bed design services for clients looking to create a more long term landscape foundation tailored to their space, sunlight, and style.

Whether it’s a quick seasonal refresh or a thoughtfully planned perennial bed, we love creating outdoor spaces that feel layered, welcoming, and beautifully put together.

We may not be a large landscaping company, and honestly, that’s part of what makes Petal Pop different. We care deeply about the details, work closely with each client, and genuinely love the creative process behind every project.

Our goal is simple

To make beautiful outdoor spaces feel easy, exciting, and personal.
